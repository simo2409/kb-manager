Icons

Phosphor Icons — a flexible icon family for interfaces, diagrams and presentations.

Setup

Include the Phosphor web font via CDN. Icons are then rendered with an <i> tag and two classes: the style prefix and the icon name.

<script src="https://unpkg.com/@phosphor-icons/web"></script>
<i class="ph ph-house"></i>
<i class="ph-fill ph-heart" style="color: var(--accent-negative)"></i>
<i class="ph-bold ph-gear"></i>

Six weights from delicate to solid: Thin, Light, Regular, Bold, Fill and Duotone.
